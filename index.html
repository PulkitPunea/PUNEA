About PUNEA

Founded in 2018, PUNEA is an independent game development studio dedicated to pushing the boundaries of interactive entertainment. Our team of passionate developers, artists, and storytellers come together to create immersive worlds that captivate players.

We believe games are more than just entertainment—they're art forms that can inspire, challenge, and bring people together. Our mission is to craft experiences that resonate long after the controller is put down.
